Safety First

Rules & Safety

LSP Paintball is built on adrenaline, but safety is our top priority. Please read our arena rules carefully before entering the field of play.

General Game Rules

Final Authority

The referee's decision is FINAL! Arguing with the referee will not be tolerated and may lead to ejection.

Positioning

Guns MUST be on the first bunker until the start whistle. During play, you cannot move positions during a stop in play.

The Whistle

You MUST NOT shoot after the referee has blown his whistle to stop play. Immediate disqualification for violators.

Getting Hit

When hit, raise your arms to signal to the referee and players. Shooting after being hit is considered cheating.

Valid Hits

Only upper arm, upper body, and head shots count. No picking paintball pellets from the ground.

Prohibitions

No physical contact. No blind firing. No point-blank or close-range shots. No external coaching allowed.

Inside the Arena

  • Masks MUST be on at all times, even after being shot. Removing masks inside the arena is grounds for immediate ejection.
  • DO NOT point guns without barrel socks at any player until the game has started.
  • No external paintball pellets allowed inside Leisure Sports Park. No Exceptions.

Outside the Arena

  • Guns must be on the gun rack AT ALL TIMES if they do not have barrel socks on them.
  • NO SHOOTING OR DRY FIRING OUTSIDE THE ARENA!! Violators will be disqualified.
  • Do not disassemble rental equipment. Draw staff attention for any difficulties.

Pellet Policy

Strict quality control for your safety.

!

NO external paintball pellets allowed. Paintball pellets can ONLY be purchased at the reception.

!

LSP previously purchased pellets must be inspected and reviewed prior to usage.

!

PAYMENTS MUST BE MADE AT THE RECEPTION BEFORE GEAR UP AND PLAY. No cash allowed.

Zero Tolerance Policy

Penalties & Equipment Misuse

Violations

Penalties for breaking rules include disqualification from one round or immediate ejection from the game without a refund.

Equipment Abuse

Misuse/abuse of equipment (throwing, slamming, or swinging guns) will not be tolerated. Players will be charged for any damages.
