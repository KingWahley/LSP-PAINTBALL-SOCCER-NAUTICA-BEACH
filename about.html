About LSP PAINTBALL & SOCCER NAUTICA BEACH

We are one of Lagos's best paintball arenas, located in a scenic setting at Nautica Beach Resort. One of the first of its kind, our facility is complemented by the ocean breeze and stunning views.

Founded on the principle of delivering unmatched excitement, LSP Paintball & Soccer provides a world-class experience creating memories forged in competition and relaxation by the coast.

What We Stand For

Safety First

Our safety protocols are non-negotiable. From mandatory masks to padded body armour and strict firing zone rules, we ensure every player returns home safe.

Bonding & Growth

LSP is the perfect place for team bonding. We foster communication, strategy, and collaboration through intense, objective-based missions.

Coastal Spirit

We embrace our unique location. The energy of the beach fuels the intensity of the game, creating a atmosphere you won't find anywhere else in Lagos.

Professional Guidance

Our team is comprised of paintball enthusiasts and professional athletes who understand the game inside and out. From the moment you gear up to the final whistle, our staff is there to provide guidance, ensure fair play, and manage your equipment.
