Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before heading to the battlefield at Nautica Beach.

What do I wear?
You can wear pretty much anything as we will provide overalls, and all protective gear [masks, neck protectors, body armor]. However, we do recommend you come with closed toe shoes and not slippers or sandals so you can move comfortably as paintball requires you to be active.

What is the age limit?
Children 15 years old and above are allowed to come and enjoy paintball. They must come with a parent/guardian and also sign the waiver.

What is Open Play?

Open Play is like pick up games of paintball. It is a mixture of players who show up without a reservation or booking. You play multiple short rounds.

Is it painful?
Paintball is a very safe sport as long as safety rules are followed. Protective gear is provided including masks, neck protectors, and body armor to minimize impact.

If I get shot, do I play again?
Yes. Once you get shot, you exit the arena and wait for the next round. As long as you still have pellets, you can continue playing multiple rounds during your session.

Can I bring food and drinks?
No, food and drinks are not allowed inside LSP. There are food and drink vendors available on the beach for your convenience before or after your session.

Can I bring my personal marker?
Yes, but we will test and inspect it to ensure it meets our safety requirements and velocity limits before you are allowed to use it in the arena.

Can I bring my own pellets?
No. Only paintball pellets purchased from LSP are allowed for safety and consistency. Our pellets are high-quality and designed specifically for our arena equipment.
